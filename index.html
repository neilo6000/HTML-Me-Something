Sculptures by the sea 2017 - Cottesloe Western Australia

Every year the City of Cottesloe showcases the popular "Sculptures by the Sea exhibition" from early to mid March (late Summer).

This collection of photos were taken by me, demonstrating some of the scupltures I observed.
